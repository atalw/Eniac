General Rules

  1. To be eligible for the winning trophy the school must participate in at least 5 out of 8 events.
  2. In case of a tie for overall position the team which has positions in more number of events shall get the trophy.
  3. All participants and guests are requested to arrive at the venue by latest 8:30 AM. Schools coming in late will not be given any extra time for the events they miss.
  4. Each school can submit only one entry per event.
  5. Schools are requested to confirm the details of the participation by completing the online Google Form latest by 24th July, 2013.
  6. Participants of Movie Presentation should send the video by latest 30th July and are also requested to bring a copy on the final day.
  7. Only the first 20 schools to register will be allowed to participate in the events, so register as soon as possible.
