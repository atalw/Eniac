Innovation

The Eniac Club believes in advancement in life through the integration of computers and it's web based applications. In this era of creativity, technology and innovation, our club endeavours to promote the integration of computer and multimedia technology at the school level.

Theme

The theme for this year is Simplicity. We have kept this theme as it would be intriguing to see the various interpretations of a word which everybody knows, but few truly care to comprehend.
