  1. Programming

    1. Eligibility: Senior (XI - XII)
    2. No. of Participants: 2
    3. Time Allotted: 90 minutes
    4. Language: C++

    Guidelines

    The participants will be required to solve a number of programs in increasing order of complexity and the first team to reach the end or the team that gets the furthest will be the winner. The participants are required to be thorough with the programming language and boast logical creativity.
    There will be a 30 minutes objective type preliminary round to find the finalist among you. Participants are requested to bring an HB pencil with eraser.

    Criteria for judgment

    1. Maximum number of correct questions attempted
    2. Maximum number of programs successfully completed
    3. Intelligent/Creative Coding

  6. Powerpoint Presentation

    1. Eligibility: Class 5 and below
    2. No. of Participants: 2
    3. Time Allotted: 45 minutes

    Guidelines

  7. Gaming

    1. Eligibility: Open
    2. No. of Participants: 2

    Guidelines

  8. Rubik's Cube Competition

    1. Eligibility: Open
    2. Maximum number of participants: 2
    3. Maximum time per solve: 7 minutes

    Guidelines

    This event is speed-solving competition of the 3x3x3 Rubik's cube. The event will consist of 3 rounds. The winners will be based on the average time of all the three rounds.
    Competitors are requested to bring their own cubes. Cubes will not be provided. Only cubes with 6 distinct colors and of size 3x3x3 will be permitted.
