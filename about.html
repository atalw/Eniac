The ENIAC Club

The official computer club of Bluebells School International which has been initiated by the students is also run by us. Originally, ENIAC means Electronic Numerator Integrator and Calculator, but this word means much more to us. According to us, it's full form is

"Encoding. Networking. Innovating. Achieving. Computing."
Overall, a high octane filled adrenaline rush powered by the brains of the members brings this club to existence.

Our previous events, VIRUS 2001, 2003 and Enter-o-Virus 2005, 2006, 2007, 2008, and 2009 were a roaring success thanks to all the participating teams. Our school has also been awarded the prestigious Intel Award for the best Integration of Technology with Education and British Council International School Award.
